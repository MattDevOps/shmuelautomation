# Phase 2 Scope — Publishing & Scheduling

**Duration**: 3-4 weeks
**Price**: $2,000 (due at phase completion)
**Prerequisite**: Phase 1 complete and signed off
**Status**: draft — revisit before Phase 2 begins (scope may refine based on Phase 1 learnings)

---

## Goal

Cut daily posting time from hours to minutes. Keep your WhatsApp number safe.

---

## What's IN this phase

### One-tap share system
- From the dashboard, click "Share" on any property
- System generates:
  - Pre-written post text (your format, customizable template)
  - Photo collage (your preferred layout)
  - Correct hashtags / links
- WhatsApp / Facebook opens pre-filled — you tap send
- Works for individual groups and your WhatsApp status
- Total time per property across all groups: ~2 minutes

### Smart scheduler
- Auto-schedules properties for posting
- Default schedule:
  - 3 properties at 8:00 AM
  - 3 properties at 8:00 PM
  - **No posts** Friday night or all day Saturday
  - Resume Saturday ~10:00 PM
- New listings added today get priority — they post first
- Older listings continue in queue order after new ones clear
- You get a notification when it's time to tap-send the scheduled posts

### Rent vs sale separation
- Different group lists for rent and for sale
- Each property type posts only to its matching groups
- System knows automatically based on the property's type field

### Configurable group lists
- Add / remove / rename groups anytime from the dashboard
- Separate lists for: Facebook-rent, Facebook-sale, WhatsApp-rent, WhatsApp-sale, WhatsApp-status
- Change groups whenever you want without redeploying anything

### Janglo posting
- If Janglo has an API: fully automated posting of rental listings
- If not: semi-automated (form pre-filled, you click submit)
- Determined during Phase 2 kickoff

## What's OUT of this phase (Phase 3 or later)

- AI chatbot on WhatsApp
- Call / WhatsApp summarization
- CRM / client tracking
- Newsletter / email
- Multi-language

---

## Explicit limitations you accept

- **No fully automated WhatsApp group posting.** One-tap share only. Protects your business number from bans.
- **No fully automated Facebook group posting.** One-tap share only. Meta's API doesn't support reliable group posting.
- **No automated WhatsApp status.** One-tap share opens status with photo + caption ready to post.
- **Yad2 imports may break** when Yad2 changes their site. Fixes come under maintenance agreement.

- Scheduler running with Shabbat rules
- Separate rent/sale queues
- Group management UI in dashboard
- Janglo posting (auto or semi, depending on API availability)
- Walkthrough / training on the posting workflow
